Of oprator:==>

Creates an observable from a sequence of values.

Example :1

data:of(tarun,kisna, abhishek)

Result

  • tarun
  • kisna
  • abhishek

Example :2

data:of({a:tarun, b:kisna, c:abhishek)

Result

  • {{ofList.a}}
  • {{ofList.b}}
  • {{ofList.c}}

from oprator:==>

The from operator creates an Observable from an array, an array-like object, a promise, an iterable object, or another Observable.

Example :1 (Array)

data:from([bahubali,devsena, kttapa])

Result

  • bahubali
  • devsena
  • kttapa

Example :2 (string to observable)

data:of('tarun')

Result

  • t
  • a
  • r
  • u
  • n
